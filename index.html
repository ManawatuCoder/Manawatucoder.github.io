Hi, I am Ben Perkins - Welcome To My Porfolio Page

This portfolio has been created to showcase my abilities as a software developer, and give you an idea of who I am.

I have always been technology focused, with my sights mostly set on computers. I have been building websites and modifying, or 'modding', video games since I was a child, and taught myself HTML at the age of twelve. Some time in early 2022 I realised I have enough understanding to, now, consider a career in I.T. and software. I have, since, been studying and building projects full time in an effort to learn and demonstrate the skills I need to enter the field.

I have multiple embedded development projects utilising microcontrollers such as the ESP32, ESP8266, and STM32 in automation and data-processing projects. As these are physical devices, I will upload videos of them working and display them on this portfolio.
